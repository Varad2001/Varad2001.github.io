Projects

Explainable Approach to Industrial Defect Detection using Computer Vision

The project classifies defective and non-defective products from their images. It also marks the region around the defect if the image is predicted defective. No information about the defect is given to the model while training, i.e., no ground truths are provided. The architecture is made such that the whole process of how the model predicts specific results and marks the defective region is explainable. The project makes use of convolutional neural networks such as VGG16 architectures.
